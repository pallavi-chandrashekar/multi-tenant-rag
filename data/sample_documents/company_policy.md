# Acme Corp -- Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements are available for roles designated as
"location-independent" and must be reviewed annually. All remote employees are
required to be reachable during core collaboration hours of 10:00–15:00 local
time and to maintain a secure, password-protected workspace.

## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 20 vacation days per year, plus 10 company holidays.
Unused vacation days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar
year. Sick leave is provided separately at 10 days per year and does not roll
over.

## Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses under $75 do not require a receipt. Expenses of $75 or more
must include an itemized receipt and be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
Reimbursements are processed in the payroll cycle following approval.

## Security and Acceptable Use
Company data must only be accessed on enrolled, encrypted devices. Sharing
customer data with external parties without a signed data processing agreement
is strictly prohibited. Suspected security incidents must be reported to the
security team within one hour of discovery.

## Code of Conduct
Acme Corp is committed to a respectful, harassment-free workplace. Violations
may be reported anonymously through the ethics hotline and are investigated
within five business days.
